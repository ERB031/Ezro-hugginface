Micro-Gamification Sneak Peek

Gentle nudges and celebratory moments that keep wellbeing playful--coming soon to Ezro's dashboard.

Prototype Concepts

Streaks for Balance

Track consecutive days of honoring quiet hours or wrapping up on time--unlock a soft glow like "3 nights safeguarding downtime."

Break Buddy Points

Accept nudges to stretch, hydrate, or breathe and build momentum. Hit 3-5 resets in a day and Ezro gently celebrates the streak.

Balance Score Progression

When workload and personal goals align, the Balance Score shifts from amber to green--an ambient "level up" without points.

Milestone Moments

Unlock subtle confetti when you complete 10 shutdown rituals or enjoy a full week with zero after-hours emails.

Weekly Reflection Badge

Complete 3+ end-of-day reflections and earn a mindful title--think "Mindful Finisher" floating next to your avatar.

"Future You" Kudos

Block buffer time and Ezro sends a note--"Future You will love this breathing room." Quiet cheers, no pressure.

Quiet Hours Protectors

Stay email-free after hours for a week and your personal "Quiet Hours Champions" list lights up--visible only to you.

Mini-Challenges

Opt-in for gentle quests like "2 standing breaks per day." Earn a soft checkmark animation when you close it out.

Team Balance Wins

Share anonymous wins--"This week, 60% of the team respected daily task limits." Celebrate the collective rhythm.

Gentle Unlockables

Keep healthy streaks alive to reveal fresh break ideas--new breathing techniques, stretch loops, and mini soundscapes.

Lightweight Pulse Library

Quick-touch prompts Ezro can surface in context to capture wellbeing signals without slowing anyone down.

Daily Workload Slider

"How's today's workload?" with Light, Balanced, or Heavy choices in one tap.

Energy Check Emoji Tap

Morning or midday prompt with quick reactions: Energized, Okay, Drained, or Tired.

Weekly Reflection Question

Friday check-in: "How balanced did your week feel?" with Good, Neutral, or Overloaded responses.

Nudge-Aware Pulse Check

If three breaks are skipped, Ezro gently asks how the user is feeling to prevent silent overwork.

Meeting Overload Tap

After a day with 5+ meetings, one-tap "Too many meetings today?" feeds back into workload insights.

Mood Shift Pulse

Rotating word set such as Focused, Stressed, Relaxed, or Rushed for extra context when optional insight helps.

End-of-Day Wrap Pulse

After the shutdown ritual, ask "How ready do you feel to switch off?" to spot boundary pressure.

Anonymous Team Polls

Quarterly cultural pulses like "Is downtime respected here?" aggregated for leadership only.

Trend Feedback Prompt

If three Heavy days appear in a row, offer to set a daily task cap or surface balancing tips.

Micro Pulse Celebrations

When balance stays steady three days running, Ezro highlights the streak: "Great stretch of balance."

Key Design Rules

  • Always optional and easy to dismiss.
  • Private by default; only aggregates surface to managers.
  • One-tap responses that work on mobile.
  • Context-aware triggers that reflect real work patterns.
